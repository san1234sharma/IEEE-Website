About IEEE

The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) is a global professional association dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity. IEEE serves as a leading authority in various fields of engineering and technology. As a professional association, IEEE offers various benefits to its members, including access to cutting-edge research, networking opportunities, career development resources, and educational programs. IEEE serves as a vibrant global community of profession.


IEEE BIT Global View

The IEEE BIT Student Branch is one of the most renowned student branch in Bangalore. The IEEE Bangalore Section is governed by the IEEE India Council, which falls under Region 10 of the IEEE Global Organization.


IEEE Bangalore Section

IEEE Bangalore Section was established in 1977 and is one of the most vibrant and dynamic IEEE Sections across the globe. Over the years the strength of the section has grown steadily and currently the Section membership stands at more than 8600. This membership is spread across various sectors and has a nice blend of Industry, Academia and Research organizations. IEEE Bangalore Section won the prestigious MGA Outstanding Large Section Award (2013 & 2019) and R10 Distinguished Large Section Award for the year 2013.
