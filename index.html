Special interest groups

Circuits

The circuits and systems society focuses on advancing the field of circuits and systems, encompassing the theory, design, and implementation of electronic circuits and systems.Our society aims to promote knowledge exchange, collaboration, and innovation in this dynamic field.

Explore

ComputerSociety

The IEEE Computer Society is a professional organization dedicated to advancing the field of computer science and technology. By being a part of the IEEE Computer Society, students and professionals can expand their network, gain access to valuable resources, and contribute to the development and application of computing technologies.

Explore

WIE

Women in Engineering (WIE) is a dedicated community within the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) that promotes the participation and advancement of women in the field of engineering. WIE strives to create an inclusive and supportive environment for women engineers, encouraging their professional growth and leadership.
